DOMINICAN REPUBLIC AT A GLANCE CAPITAL Santo Domingo LANGUAGE Spanish CURRENCY Peso (DOP) EXTENSION 48,442 sq km POPULATION 10,075,045 (2016 estimate) Exploring Dominican Culture The Dominican Republic has a variety of landscapes, from deserts to alpine forests in the central mountains. Discover the highest mountain and deepest valley in the Caribbean, as well as rainforests, deserts, and white sand beaches! Dominican culture is a mixture of the cultures of Spaniard colonists, African slaves, and Taíno natives. European, African, and Taíno cultural elements are most prominent in Dominican food, family structure, religion, and music.
